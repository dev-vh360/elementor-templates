== Help Center Support Form — Mail Template Configuration ==

Configure these settings in the "Mail" tab of your CF7 form.

------------------------------------------------------------------

TO:
[your-email-goes-here]
(Replace with your actual support email address)

FROM:
[your-name] <[your-email]>

SUBJECT:
[your-site] Help Center Support Request - [issue-type]

ADDITIONAL HEADERS:
Reply-To: [your-name] <[your-email]>

MESSAGE BODY:
---
From: [your-name] <[your-email]>
Username: [your-username]
Issue Type: [issue-type]
Page / Feature: [page-feature]

Message:
[your-message]

---
This message was sent from the Help Center contact form on [your-site]

------------------------------------------------------------------

CONFIRMATION MESSAGE (shown to user after submission):
Thank you for contacting us! We have received your support request and will respond within 24–48 hours.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Notes:
- [your-site] is automatically replaced by CF7 with your site name
- The Reply-To header lets you reply directly to the user's email
- You may add additional notification recipients by CC-ing in the headers
