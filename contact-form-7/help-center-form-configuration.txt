== Help Center Support Form — Contact Form 7 Configuration ==

Paste this code into the "Form" tab when creating your CF7 form.
Name the form: Help Center Support Form

------------------------------------------------------------------

<label> Your Name (required)
    [text* your-name placeholder "Enter your name"] </label>

<label> Email Address (required)
    [email* your-email placeholder "Enter your email address"] </label>

<label> Username or Profile Name
    [text your-username placeholder "Your Videohub360 username"] </label>

<label> Issue Type (required)
    [select* issue-type "Account Access" "Profile Setup" "Community Features" "Live Streaming" "Session Join Issue" "Messages & Notifications" "Content Uploads" "Technical Support" "General Question"] </label>

<label> Page or Feature Related To
    [select page-feature "Dashboard" "Activity Feed" "Members Directory" "Profile Page" "Direct Messages" "Live Room" "Content Upload" "Mobile / PWA" "Other"] </label>

<label> Describe Your Issue (required)
    [textarea* your-message placeholder "Please describe the issue you are experiencing in as much detail as possible..."] </label>

[submit "Submit Support Request"]

------------------------------------------------------------------
Notes:
- Fields marked with * are required
- The issue-type and page-feature dropdowns help route tickets efficiently
- The your-username field is optional but helps the team locate the account
